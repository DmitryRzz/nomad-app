# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 24, 2026**

## Introduction

NOMAD AI Travel Planner ("NOMAD", "we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information
- **Email address**: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Name**: Optional display name for your profile
- **Password**: Encrypted and stored securely using industry-standard hashing

### Travel Data
- **Routes and itineraries**: Your created trips, destinations, and preferences
- **Location data**: Only when you explicitly use the Smart Compass feature
- **Saved places**: Points of interest you bookmark or visit

### Device Information
- **Device ID**: For push notifications and session management
- **FCM Token**: Firebase Cloud Messaging token for trip reminders

### Usage Analytics (Anonymous)
- Feature usage patterns (aggregated and anonymized)
- Crash reports for app stability improvements

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Provide Services**: Generate AI routes, save itineraries, offline access
2. **Personalization**: Tailor recommendations based on your travel preferences
3. **Notifications**: Send trip reminders and route updates (only with permission)
4. **Improvement**: Analyze usage to enhance AI algorithms and app features

## Data Storage and Security

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted via HTTPS; passwords hashed with bcrypt
- **Local Storage**: Routes saved on your device for offline access
- **Server Storage**: Encrypted at rest on our servers
- **Retention**: Account data retained until deletion request; anonymized analytics kept for 12 months

## Third-Party Services

We use the following services:
- **Firebase**: Push notifications and analytics
- **OpenAI / Groq / DeepSeek**: AI route generation (data anonymized)
- **Apple App Store / Google Play**: App distribution and in-app purchases

## Your Rights

You can:
- **Access** your data at any time through the app
- **Delete** your account and all associated data via Profile > Delete Account
- **Opt-out** of push notifications in device settings
- **Export** your routes (coming in a future update)

## Children's Privacy

NOMAD is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or in-app notice.
